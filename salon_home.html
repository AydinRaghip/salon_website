About us


Nestled in the heart of Highams Park, Daniell Salon is a modern and creative space where you can sit back, relax and enjoy the full Salon experience. The Daniell Salon & Academy team offer a wealth of knowledge and expertise which is sure to blow your mind. Our main aim is to deliver an exclusive service to each individual client, making them feel like no other. As a salon, we are all about healthy hair. With our experienced stylists, we can advise and guide you down the right path to amazing hair. Our master colourists will be sure to work their magic on you. We offer the latest cutting-edge styles alongside complimentary consultations to put your mind completely at ease throughout the whole process. We are also offering people of all abilities the opportunity to attend our private courses at our academy. Whether you have no experience in Hairdressing or whether you are a fully qualified hair stylist looking to further your knowledge and experience, we could have the course you're looking for! All courses at Daniell Salon & Academy are delivered by a fully qualified teacher and assessor who has over 6 years teaching experience and 18 years experience in the hairdressing industry. Every idea we create is with the client and student in mind, so, you're in safe hands with us.
